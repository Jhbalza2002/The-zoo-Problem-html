List of the current animals of the Zoo

Bears

Bears are large, powerful mammals with shaggy fur, short tails, and strong limbs. They are omnivores, with diets ranging from berries and fish to small mammals.
Known for their ability to stand on two legs, bears inhabit diverse environments, from forests to arctic regions. They are solitary creatures, except for mothers with cubs.

Giraffes

Giraffes are the tallest land animals, recognized by their long necks and legs, and distinctive coat patterns. Native to African savannas, they primarily feed on
leaves from tall trees, especially acacias. Giraffes have excellent eyesight and a calm demeanor, moving gracefully despite their height.

Lions

Lions are powerful, social big cats with a tawny coat and males having a distinctive mane. They live in prides, consisting of related females and a few males.
Native to Africa, lions are apex predators, hunting large ungulates. Known for their strength and majestic roars, they symbolize courage and nobility.
